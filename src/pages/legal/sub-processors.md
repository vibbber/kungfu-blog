# Sub-Processors

**Last Updated:** April 1, 2026

This page lists the third-party service providers ("sub-processors") that process data on behalf of Vibbber Tech FCZO in connection with the Kung Fu Family platform.

We maintain Data Processing Agreements (DPAs) with all sub-processors that process personal data on our behalf, ensuring they are contractually bound to protect your data in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

---

## AI Processing

These providers process child profile data and conversation content to generate personalized parenting guidance. All AI processing is performed via API with zero data retention enabled. Child profile data and conversation content are processed in real-time and not stored by these providers for model training or any other purpose beyond generating the immediate response.

| Provider | Service | Data Processed | Location |
|----------|---------|---------------|----------|
| Google (Gemini) | AI language model | Child profile data, conversation content | United States |
| Anthropic (Claude) | AI language model | Child profile data, conversation content | United States |
| OpenAI | AI language model | Child profile data, conversation content | United States |
| xAI (Grok) | AI language model | Child profile data, conversation content | United States |

*We may use one or more of these providers depending on the task. Not all providers are used for every request.*
