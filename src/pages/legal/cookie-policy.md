# Cookie Policy

**Effective Date:** April 1, 2026
**Last Updated:** April 1, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Vibbber Tech FCZO ("Vibbber Tech," "we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on the Kung Fu Family platform, including app.kungfu.family and kungfu.family (the "Service").

---

## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website or use an application. They help the site remember your preferences, understand how you use it, and improve your experience.

---

## Types of Cookies We Use

### Essential Cookies

**Purpose:** Required for the Service to function. These cannot be disabled.

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| Session token | Keeps you logged in | Session / 30 days |
| CSRF token | Protects against cross-site request forgery | Session |
| Cookie consent | Remembers your cookie preferences | 12 months |

### Analytics Cookies

**Purpose:** Help us understand how users interact with the Service so we can improve it. These are only set with your consent.

| Cookie | Provider | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|----------|---------|----------|
| `_ga` | Google Analytics | Distinguishes unique users | 2 years |
| `_ga_*` | Google Analytics | Maintains session state | 2 years |
| `_gid` | Google Analytics | Identifies user within a 24-hour session | 24 hours |

**What analytics data includes:** Pages visited, time on page, device type, general geographic region (country/city level from IP), referring source, feature usage patterns.

**What analytics data does NOT include:** Your name, email, child profile data, conversation content, or any personally identifying information.

### Functional Cookies

**Purpose:** Remember your preferences and settings to provide a more personalized experience. These are only set with your consent.

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| Theme preference | Remembers light/dark mode selection | 12 months |
| Language preference | Remembers your language setting | 12 months |

## Your Choices

### Cookie Consent Banner

When you first visit the Service, you will see a cookie consent banner. You can:

- **Accept all cookies** — Essential, analytics, and functional cookies are enabled
- **Essential only** — Only cookies required for the Service to function are enabled
- **Manage preferences** — Choose which optional cookie categories to enable

### Changing Your Preferences

You can change your cookie preferences at any time by:

- Clicking the cookie settings link in the Service footer
- Clearing your browser's cookies (this will reset your consent choice)

### Browser Settings

Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies through their settings. Note that blocking essential cookies may prevent the Service from functioning properly.

## Global Privacy Control and Do Not Track

### Global Privacy Control (GPC)

We honor Global Privacy Control signals. When we detect a GPC signal (`Sec-GPC: 1` header or `navigator.globalPrivacyControl`), we disable all non-essential cookies and treat the signal as a valid opt-out of the sale or sharing of personal information, as required by applicable state privacy laws.

### Do Not Track (DNT)

Some browsers send a "Do Not Track" signal. When we detect a DNT signal, we disable non-essential cookies for that session, equivalent to selecting "Essential only."
